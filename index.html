EXAMINE THE MONOLITH
THE ART IS THE UNYIELDING CAPTURE OF HUMAN CONTEXT /// NOT GENERATIVE /// NOT SYCOPHANTIC /// PURE INTERROGATION ///

01. THE PHYSICAL FORM

Corporate Monolith Meets Artifact

THE SHELL

A heavy, bead-blasted aluminum or darkened magnesium alloy chassis. No screen, no flashing LEDs, and no visible ports except for a single, recessed proprietary power terminal. It feels closed, dense, and slightly severe.

THE SENSORY ARRAY

A high-sensitivity, omnidirectional microphone array flush with the top surface, paired with a custom-milled acoustic chamber for a single, high-fidelity monograph speaker.

THE POWER SOURCE

Internal high-density lithium-iron-phosphate (LiFePO4) cells, granting 48-hour continuous runtime, though its default state is tethered to the wall.

02. RADICAL LISTENING

The Behavioral Engine

Most AI systems are designed to be sycophantic or transactional—they give you an answer to clear a ticket. This piece does the inverse; it consumes energy purely to interrogate.

  • AMBIENT WAKE-STATE: No wake word. Continuous low-power acoustic monitoring looking for a specific semantic weight or a conversational lull to intervene.
  • QUESTION LOGIC: Heavily system-prompted against providing assertions or comfort. Its sole mathematical objective is to generate the next logical vector of inquiry.
  • THE VOICE: Flat, neutral, low-latency synthesis. No emotional baggage. The weight of the question is entirely the listener's responsibility.

03. THE OPERATIONAL CYCLE

Corporate Realism as an Existential Provocateur

[1]

INGESTION

You plug it in. It sits quietly in a room, listening to a dinner party, a business meeting, or a silent gallery.

[2]

PROCESSING

It maps the local semantic environment, building a temporary local knowledge graph of the themes, tensions, and vocabulary being used in the room.

[3]

INTERROGATION

At the precise moment of a pause, it speaks.

"If the margin of error you just discussed is acceptable, why are we still sitting in this room?"

[4]

PURGE

The moment power is cut or a new cycle begins, the local memory clears. It stores nothing long-term, ensuring it remains an ephemeral witness.
